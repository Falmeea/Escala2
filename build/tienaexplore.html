Blurb

Tiena's childhood spent traversing the land beneath starlit skies comes to a harsh standstill after her mother's tragic passing in an alchemical mishap. A year later, she is struggling to adapt to the bustling Dwarven Capital of Diyun. Its loud, rude and terribly windy; her placement in a class with Dwarves half her age only deepen her sense of isolation. Tiena’s only solace is the fleeting visits from the Twins once a week. With her father buried in work and her uncle obsessed with his collections, Tiena feels utterly alone her new luxury prison.

But Tiena's mundane troubles take a fantastical turn when a smelly ratboy —one of the Veremon no less, —appears at her window, fleeing from the clutches of a vicious underground gang; Tiena takes the chance at another life, and dares to plunge headfirst into an adventure that flips her world on its head, into one brimming with magic and danger.

Though that really isn’t much of a surprise, not only does Tiena have her mother’s fiery temperament, she, is also a very special child. A web of secrets surrounds her very existence, woven by her mother and kept by her father in a desperate bid to protect her from the shadows that ensnare their family. Unbeknownst to Tiena, she is central to a whirlwind of ancient forces poised to unveil realities, and finally answer the most important question of all: What is her father working on?

Be there to watch Tiena make friends, grow and make mistakes that will haunt you.
